## 1. THE VALUE EQUATION — 4 Variables (Hormozi)

```
Perceived Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Probability of Achievement)
                  ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

**Optimization objective for $17-$47 digital products:**
Do NOT promise to change the buyer's entire life. Target one surgical micro-transformation
on ONE precise problem. The more specific the promise, the higher the believability.

---

### Variable 1 — DREAM OUTCOME

**Rule:** Anchored on verbatim language from `09-buyer-psychology.json.verbatims_bank`.
Never invented. The exact transformation the buyer describes in their own words.

**Format:** `"[Specific Result] in [Precise Time] without [Primary Obstacle from persona]"`

**Examples (calibrated by price point):**
| Price | ❌ Too Vague | ✅ Calibrated Promise |
|-------|-------------|----------------------|
| $17 | "Lose weight" | "Drop 8 lbs in 21 days without giving up wine or pasta" |
| $27 | "Make money online" | "$500 from your first digital product in 30 days, even with zero audience" |
| $37 | "Save more money" | "Build a $1,000 emergency fund in 90 days on your current salary" |
| $47 | "Get organized" | "Free 10 hours/week from your schedule in 14 days using one Notion dashboard" |

**Believability Threshold rule:**
- Bolder promise → irrefutable proof required
- Frame exceptional results as "possibilities documented" not "guaranteed universals"
- One strategic flaw admitted = credibility of benefits multiplied

---

### Variable 2 — PERCEIVED PROBABILITY

Three simultaneous levers (deploy all three):

**Lever A — Guarantee (see guarantee-scarcity.md for full types)**
- Best for $17-$47: Keep-Product or Better-Than-Money-Back
- 60-day window surpasses 30-day by +23% conversion without increasing refunds

**Lever B — Proximity Rule (Copyhackers research)**
- Place 1-3 short testimonials within 2.5cm (visual proximity) of primary CTA button
- Impact: +15% to +34% conversion lift
- ICP Matching Rule: testimonials must match persona from skill 09 exactly
  ("If it worked for someone exactly like me…")
- Ideal format: Name, specific result, timeframe
  → "Sarah, 38, mom of 3: Lost 11 lbs in 28 days. Still ate pizza every Friday."

**Lever C — Proof Block architecture**
- Place proof BEFORE price reveal — never after
- Sequence: Result claim → Proof → Price (never Price → Proof)
- Combine social proof + scarcity: "847 bought this month. 12 spots left for [Bonus]."

---

### Variable 3 — TIME DELAY

**Rule:** Time-to-Value < 24 hours.

Module 1 / Chapter 1 must generate a VISIBLE WIN the same day as purchase.
This single rule reduces refund requests by eliminating buyer's remorse window.

**Quick Win templates by niche:**
| Niche | Quick Win (Day 0) |
|-------|-------------------|
| Fitness | Complete 20-min workout from Module 1, log first measurement |
| Finance | Set up automated transfer in 10 min using provided template |
| Productivity | Clear inbox to zero using the 3-box method (Lesson 1) |
| Digital biz | Publish first piece of content using the plug-and-play template |

**How to describe in output:**
```json
"time_delay": "Chapter 1 'The 20-Minute Trigger' delivers first measurable result
within 4 hours of purchase — before bedtime on Day 0."
```

---

### Variable 4 — EFFORT & SACRIFICE

**Rule:** Plug-and-play assets are mandatory. They are the #1 psychological bonus category.

**What constitutes "plug-and-play":**
- Notion/Google Sheet templates → open, duplicate, use immediately
- Copy-paste scripts → no adaptation needed
- Step-by-step checklists → one checkbox at a time, no thinking required
- AI prompts → paste into ChatGPT, get result in 60 seconds
- Swipe files → steal and deploy same day

**The framing rule:**
The product is NOT a "course you study". It IS a "system you execute."
Semantic velocity words: "plug-and-play", "copy-paste", "done-for-you", "just duplicate",
"steal this", "ready in 60 seconds".

## 2. VALUE STACK — Construction Rules

### Hierarchy (from most to least psychologically powerful)

| Rank | Type | Why It Works | Examples |
|------|------|--------------|---------|
| 1 | **Plug-and-Play** | Eliminates execution friction immediately | Templates, scripts, swipe files, AI prompts |
| 2 | **Speed & Shortcuts** | "Get there faster" — activates time value | 7-day plan, quick-start guide, shortcuts doc |
| 3 | **Accountability** | Certainty of implementation | Private community, live Q&A, audit session |
| 4 | **Exclusivity** | Belonging & FOMO | Behind-the-scenes access, early bird, vault |
| 5 | **Financial** | Weakest — easily discounted mentally | Discount codes, cash rebates |

**Priority rule:** Build the stack from Rank 1 downward. Never lead with financial.
If forced to include a financial bonus, bury it at the bottom of the stack presentation.

---

### VALUE ATTRIBUTION — Pricing the Stack

**Method: Consultant Equivalent Pricing**
1. Ask: "What would a specialist charge to deliver this result?"
2. That hourly/project rate = perceived value of the bonus
3. Cross-reference with market equivalents for credibility

**Examples:**
- Notion template pack (saves 8h setup) → freelancer rate $75/h × 8 = $597 perceived value
- Private community (12-month access, moderated) → equivalent Slack community membership = $97/yr
- 30-min accountability call → consultant rate $150/30min
- Swipe file (50 proven subject lines) → copywriter hourly $200 × 3h research = $97 perceived value

**Rule:** The proof dictates the promise — never the inverse.
Do not assign inflated values that cannot be justified.

## 3. THE STACK — Presentation Technique (Brunson)

**Rule:** Never reveal total price before building the full stack.

**Presentation sequence:**
1. Introduce Core Offer → state perceived value → "But that's not all…"
2. Add Bonus 1 → running total → "But I'm also including…"
3. Add Bonus 2 → running total → "And if you act in the next 48 hours…"
4. Reveal Fast-Action Bonus → running total
5. Reveal Guarantee → "And here's my promise to you…"
6. Reveal total stack value (high number)
7. Introduce price ($47) as contrast to total → massive perceived value gap

**Copy template for offer reveal:**
```
"So here's everything you get today:
The [Core Offer Name] — a $[X] value
[Bonus 1 Name] — a $[X] value
[Bonus 2 Name] — a $[X] value
[Fast-Action Bonus] — a $[X] value (only for the next 48 hours)
Plus my [N]-day [Guarantee Type] guarantee — worth $[X] in peace of mind

Total value: $[Total]
Today's price: just $[Price]

And if it doesn't [Core Promise]... you pay nothing."
```

## 4. FAST-ACTION BONUS — Rules

**Mechanism:** Deadline-linked bonus (48-72 hours from opt-in or launch).

**Critical rule:** If deadline expires → product remains, ONLY the fast-action bonus disappears.
This triggers loss aversion WITHOUT punishing late buyers (ethical scarcity).

**Tech implementation:** Deadline Funnel (IP + cookie + email tracking).
Never use page-refresh countdown timers — 2026 buyers detect these instantly.

**Copy framing:**
```
"⚡ FAST-ACTION BONUS (expires [Day/Time]):
[Bonus Name] — [Specific Benefit]
→ Available for the next [N] hours only
→ If you're reading this after [Deadline], this bonus is gone — but everything else still applies."
```

## 5. UNIQUE MECHANISM — Naming Rules

**Why it matters:** A named mechanism makes competitive comparison impossible.
The buyer can only evaluate YOUR system on YOUR terms.

**Naming formula options:**
- `The [Adjective] [Noun] [Method/System/Protocol]`
  → "The Surgical Visibility Protocol"
  → "The 48-Hour Revenue Engine"
  → "The Invisible Offer Framework"

**Naming constraints:**
- Must be 2-5 words
- Must sound proprietary (not a generic phrase)
- Must connect semantically to the core promise
- Should NOT use competitor terminology

**Description structure (3 sentences):**
1. What it is in plain language
2. Why it works differently than conventional approaches
3. What specific outcome it enables (tethered to core promise)

## 6. BIG DOMINO — Identification Rules

**Source:** Must come from `09-buyer-psychology.json.advanced_tactics.big_domino_shift`.
NEVER invented by skill 12.

**What it is:** The ONE limiting belief that, if collapsed, makes every other objection irrelevant.

**Guilt-Shift Method (source: skill 09 doctrine):**
```
Phase 1 (Push): Agitate frustration with current approach
Phase 2 (Externalize): Prove failure is systemic, not personal
  → "You didn't fail because you lack discipline. You failed because [Old System]
     was designed for someone with [Resources/Time] you don't have."
Phase 3 (Pull): New mechanism as the logical bridge out
```

**Output format:**
```json
"big_domino_claim": "The reason you've struggled with [X] isn't willpower or knowledge —
it's that every approach you've tried required [Unreasonable Friction]. The [Unique Mechanism]
eliminates this variable entirely."
```
