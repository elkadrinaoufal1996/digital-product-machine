# Elimination Filters — Skill 01

Reference for binary exclusion of signals before niche clustering.
Used exclusively by Skill 01. Do not duplicate in other skills.

---

## Filter 1 — LEGAL

**Trigger:** Any niche requiring regulated professional advice.

Excluded categories:
- Medical diagnosis, treatment, or prescription claims
- Financial advice requiring SEC/FINRA licensing (investment strategies, portfolio management)
- Legal services (contract drafting, legal advice)
- Dietary supplements with medical claims (FDA gray zone)
- Therapy or clinical psychology services

**Not excluded:**
- Personal finance budgeting (no investment advice)
- Wellness and lifestyle (no medical claims)
- Fitness and movement (no clinical claims)

---

## Filter 2 — SATURATED

**Trigger:** Niche has no exploitable gap.

Criteria (ALL must be true to eliminate):
1. 3+ established actors with >10,000 reviews on Amazon Kindle
2. Top 3 Gumroad sellers in category have >5,000 sales each
3. No visible gap in BSR top 10 (format, audience, or angle)

**Important:** Saturation without gap ≠ mere competition. A saturated niche WITH an identifiable UVZ is NOT eliminated — it is flagged [BORDERLINE].

---

## Filter 3 — NO-PAY

**Trigger:** Audience consumes content for free but does not purchase.

Evidence required to eliminate:
- Zero Gumroad products with >100 sales in the niche
- Amazon Kindle BSR > 50,000 for all comparable titles
- No paid courses, memberships, or ebooks identified in the niche

**Note:** TikTok views alone are NOT sufficient to pass this filter.

---

## Filter 4 — SEASONAL

**Trigger:** Demand is structurally tied to a single calendar event.

Examples: Christmas gift guides, Valentine's Day, Tax season (if no evergreen angle), Back-to-school.

**Not eliminated if:** seasonal spike exists but evergreen demand is documented year-round.

---

## Filter 5 — PLATFORM-RISK

**Trigger:** Niche viability depends 100% on a single platform that could moderate or deplatform the creator.

Example: Niches exclusively viable via TikTok Shop, OnlyFans, or a single social algorithm.

**Not eliminated if:** multi-channel distribution is viable (email list, own website, Gumroad).

---

## Borderline Flag

Tag [BORDERLINE] (do not eliminate) when:
- Niche passes all 5 filters but shows weakness on 2+ of the following:
  - Demand signal from only 1 source (not 2+)
  - UVZ is weak or unclear
  - No-pay risk is present but not conclusive
  - Saturated in one sub-segment but open in another

Borderline niches are passed to Skill 02 with a lower priority weight applied automatically.
